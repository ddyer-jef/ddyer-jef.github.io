Where to sit in a canoe

Before you just jump into a canoe all willy-nilly, there are some things you should have figured out, like where the bow (front) of the boat is and where the stern (back) is. You should also figure out who's sitting where. The easiest way to figure out where the bow of the boat is, is to look at the seats.
